msbt_xp is a simple addon that adds extra experience gain related 
information to msbt (Mik's Scrolling Battle Text)

<b>Each time you kill an NPC the addon will report:</b>
 * Experience Current / Experience Total
 * Percentage of level completed
 * Kills needed to level
